Never lose track of your orders

Ordered gifts from multiple stores? Waiting on deliveries? Wrapped Up keeps everything organized so you know exactly what's arriving, what needs wrapping, and what's ready to give.

Track every step

Mark each gift's status: Idea → Ordered → Received → Wrapped. Always know what's still in transit, what just arrived, and what's ready under the tree.

Add important details

Track order numbers, delivery dates, store locations, or where you hid that wrapped present. Notes keep the crucial details at your fingertips.

Manage gifts for everyone

Create profiles for family, friends, and colleagues. See all their gifts at a glance and never wonder "did I already buy something for Mom?" Stay in control whether you're managing holiday shopping for a big family or just tracking a few birthday presents.

Unlock Unlimited Gift Tracking

Upgrade to Pro for unlimited people and gifts, detailed notes and links, and custom statuses to make the app truly yours.
